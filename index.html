About ESCI-2026

The 8th IEEE International Conference on Emerging Smart Computing and Informatics (ESCI-2026) is a platform where academics and industry researchers present their latest algorithms, models, and technologies, and explore new trends and ideas in smart computing and informatics.

The aim of this conference is to provide a unified platform for emerging trends in smart computing, networking, communication, signal and image processing, cybersecurity, and IoT.

The key intention is to provide an opportunity for global participants to share their ideas and experiences in person with peers expected to join from different parts of the world. The conference will help delegates to establish a research environment and find international linkages for future collaborations in their career paths.

The conference focuses on various modernization and innovation paradigms that can be applied to present practical and creative solutions to diverse issues in society, industry, and the environment.

The conference proceedings invite academics and industry researchers to publish papers explaining literature reviews, problem statements, and methodologies with appropriate results. Selected papers will be sent to experts for review. Acceptance will be based on significance, innovation, originality, and the quality of unpublished work. Accepted and presented papers will be published in the IEEE Xplore Digital Library.

The conference also invites papers on special topics, tools, and applications in multidisciplinary areas, along with tutorial sessions conducted by expert academicians and professionals.
